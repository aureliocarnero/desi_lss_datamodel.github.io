coordinates-EXPID¶

Summary:

Coordinates data

Naming Convention:

coordinates-EXPID.fits, where EXPID is the zero-padded 8-digit exposure ID.

Regex:

coordinates-[0-9]{8}\.fits

File Type:

FITS, 1 MB
