zmtl-SPECTROGRAPH-TILEID-thruGROUPID.fits

Summary:

The zmtl files contain information about redshifts used by the MTL (“Merged Target List”) process to update the observational state of targets in the MTL ledgers. In particular, the redshift information is crucial for deciding whether a target is a Lyman-alpha quasar that requires additional DESI observations.

Naming Convention:

zmtl-SPECTROGRAPH-TILEID-thruGROUPID.fits, where SPECTROGRAPH is the spectrograph ID, TILEID is the tile number and GROUPID depends on the GROUPTYPE of the tile coadd.

Regex:

zmtl-[0-9]-[0-9]+-(thru|)[0-9]{8}\.fits

File Type:

FITS, 67 KB

Only either the four SV1, SV3 or Main Survey columns will be present. TARGET bitmask columns are preceded by the survey PHASE except in the case of Main Survey files (i.e. DESI_TARGET is called SV1_DESI_TARGET when the survey PHASE is sv1).

Notes and Examples

See the DESI Survey Operations paper (Schlafly et al., in preparation) for details of how the quantities in the zmtl files are used to update the observational state of a target in the MTL ledgers.

For more information, see QuasarNET for QuasarNET and SQUEzE for SQUEzE.
