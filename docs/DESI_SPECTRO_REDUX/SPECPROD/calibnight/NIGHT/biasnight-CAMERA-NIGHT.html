biasnight-CAMERA-NIGHT.fits

Summary:

Master bias frame for the preprocessing of the night data

Naming Convention:

biasnight-CAMERA-NIGHT.fits.gz, where CAMERA is e.g., “b0”, “r5”, etc. and NIGHT is the observation night in YYYYMMDD format.

Regex:

biasnight-[brz][0-9]-[0-9]{8}\.fits\.gz

File Type:

FITS, 20 MB
