Potential assigment files

Summary:

This file includes all reachable sky coordinates, as determined by the DESI fiberassign software, of randoms matching the footprint of DESI target samples as well as information about collisions

Naming Convention:

pota-{OBSCON}.fits or pota-{OBSCON}_{TAG}_{TRACER}.fits, where {OBSCON} can be DARK or BRIGHT and {TAG} can be joined_unreduced or withntile and {TRACER} can be LRG, QSO, ELG_LOP, LRG+ELG_LOP for DARK or BGS for BRIGHT

Regex:

pota-(BRIGHT|DARK)_(joined_unreduced|withntile)_(LRG|BGS|QSO|ELG)\.fits

File Type:

FITS, 2 GB
