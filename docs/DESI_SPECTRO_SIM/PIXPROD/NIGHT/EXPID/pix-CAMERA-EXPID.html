pix-CAMERA-EXPID.fits¶

Summary:

Pre-processed CCD pixel data.

Naming Convention:

pix-{CAMERA}-{EXPID}.fits, where {CAMERA} is the camera name (e.g. b0, r1, or z9), and {EXPID} is the 8-digit exposure ID.

Regex:

pix-[brz]{1}[0-9]{1}-[0-9]{8}\.fits

File Type:

FITS, 320 MB
