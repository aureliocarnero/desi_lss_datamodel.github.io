ZenithExtinction¶

Summary:

Atmospheric extinction data.

Naming Convention:

ZenithExtinction-{observatory}.fits, where {observatory} is the name of the name of the observatory where the extinction was measured, e.g. ‘KPNO’.

Regex:

ZenithExtinction-[A-Z]+\.fits

File Type:

FITS, 1 MB
