rancomb_wdupspec¶

Summary:

Match of randoms with information from the spectroscopic and target sample.

Naming Convention:

rancomb_{RANN}{PROGRAM}wdupspec_zdone.fits, where {RANN} is the number of the random file (0 through 17) and {PROGRAM} is the observing program, either dark or bright.

Regex:

rancomb_[0-17][a-z]{4,6}wdupspec_Alltiles.fits

File Type:

FITS, 1 GB
