sframe-CAMERA-EXPID.fits¶

Summary:

fiber-flatfielded and sky-subtracted but not flux calibrated per-camera spectra.

Naming Convention:

sframe-{CAMERA}-{EXPID}.fits, where {CAMERA} is one of the spectrograph cameras (e.g. z1) and {EXPID} is the 8-digit exposure ID.

Regex:

sframe-[brz][0-9]-[0-9]{8}\.fits

File Type:

FITS, 70 MB

FITS Header Units¶

HDU0¶

EXTNAME = FLUX

2D array of fiber flat-fielded and sky subtracted flux of dimension [nspec, nwave] in units of electrons per Angstrom. nspec is the number of fibers per camera. nwave in the length of the wavelength array. The spectra of all fibers share the same wavelength grid (given in HDU WAVELENGTH). sframe.flux = frame.flux / flatfield - sky .
