mtl-OBSCON-hp-HPX

Summary:

MTL ledgers for the DESI bright-time program.

Naming Convention:

mtl-OBSCON-hp-HPX.ecsv, where HPX is the nside=32 (NESTED) HEALPixel integer and OBSCON can be either dark or bright.

Regex:

mtl-[a-z]+-hp-[0-9]+.ecsv

File Type:

ecsv, 0-10 MB
