datcomb_tarspecwdup_Alltiles¶

Summary:

Match of targets (with duplicates after FA) with spectroscopic data from the specprod (fuji/guadalupe for SV3/DA02).

Naming Convention:

datcomb_{PROGRAM}_tarspecwdup_Alltiles.fits, where {PROGRAM} denotes the observing program, either dark or bright.

Regex:

datcomb_[a-z]{4,6}_tarspecwdup_Alltiles.fits

File Type:

FITS, 2 GB
